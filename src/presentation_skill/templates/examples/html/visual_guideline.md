# Reveal Deck Visual Guideline

## Composition owner

HTML/CSS owns slide geometry, exact copy, numbers, links, and interaction. Generated assets may supply textless icons or illustrations inside bounded visual containers.

## Asset grammar

- Flat 2D line art with transparent backgrounds
- One shared line weight and visual density
- Amber for structure; red only for factual failure
- No generated text, logos, QR codes, screenshots, or quantitative charts
- Generated assets align through `.card-visual`, not per-slide inline offsets

## Distance and density

- One claim per slide
- No more than four peer containers without a strong reason
- Body text stays readable from the back of a room
- At 50% scale, the claim must read before the icon
